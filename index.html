About Us

Myinsurans.com is Malaysia's leading online insurance comparison portal that has – since its inception in June, 2015 – earned for itself the trust and goodwill of our customers seeking both life and non-life insurance.

Our vision is to empower the Malaysian consumers, help them compare and make an informed choice for their various insurance needs.

Started with a team of three first-time entrepreneurs, today we have a 1,500+-strong team committed in providing the best-in-class insurance solutions to Malaysian consumers. We owe our success to our comparison engine which is the result of intense research and periodic updation of data. Here, we help you find the insurance plan that best suits your needs, after factoring in the features and benefits you wish to avail, at a price you can afford.
